I had my first experiences creating websites in 2006 when I taught myself HTML and CSS in order to create a website for an organisation. Even though the website was never published, I remained fascinated with the possibilities this technology offers. However it was not until my time in university studying philosophy that I started to systematically acquire the skills required in web development with a serious conviction.

During the last years of my BA program I started dedicating more and more time towards learning web technologies and design principles. The year I graduated, I started freelancing and building websites for clients.

Being an autodidact and constantly upgrading my skills has been a lot of hard work but I enjoy developing and improving websites, user experiences and learning and applying new technologies. In the years to come I aim to continue learning new skills constantly, keep creating websites and collaborate with those who share my interest.
